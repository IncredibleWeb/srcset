Reload the page at a different viewport width by resizing your window and notice the different image that is downloaded.

Optimal

<img src="images/sample-image-320.jpg" data-srcset="images/sample-image-640.jpg 321w, images/sample-image-1024.jpg 641w, images/sample-image-1920.jpg 1025w" alt="sample-image">

The above image settings are optimal, i.e. it will always update the image if the viewport is larger than the image.

Conservative

<img src="images/sample-image-320.jpg" data-srcset="images/sample-image-640.jpg 640w, images/sample-image-1024.jpg 1024w, images/sample-image-1920.jpg 1920w" alt="sample-image">

The above image settings are conservative, i.e. it will only update the image when it fits within the viewport.
